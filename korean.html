Korean Novels

Here you can see my favorite novels which come from Korea. I like korean novels a lot because their culture resembles the european one the most in my opinion (out of Japan, China and Korea) and because their topics are more diverse. Though there is one particular big trend which i would call "Gaming Novels". Basically a novel where the protagonist is transfered into a gaming world or there are gaming elements in the story
